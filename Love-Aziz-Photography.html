Why us?

Love Aziz Photography is owned and operated by Latoya Parra, an event photographer based in SoCal. Her approach is simple: When looking back on your album, you need to be able to relive each moment in the most authentic way possible. You deserve it. You can expect a confident, calm and organized photographic experience! We'll be capturing the beautiful story of love and life, as it is, to be remembered and cherished forever!

What to Expect:

Prepare to have a good time! Focus on creating an experience and filling the time with moments of raw emotions. Don't worry about posing, worry about being in the moment with the ones you love. We'll handle capturing the memories that will live on forever!
